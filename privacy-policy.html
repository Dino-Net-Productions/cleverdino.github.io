Privacy Policy

At DinoNet Productions, we prioritize the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information DinoBot collects, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect your data.

Information We Collect

DinoBot collects minimal information necessary to provide its services. This includes:

  • **User Messages**: To facilitate 24/7 cross-server chatting, DinoBot temporarily stores messages to ensure seamless communication.
  • **Server Information**: Basic server data is collected to enable multi-platform chatting and AI chatbot integration.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected by DinoBot is used to:

  • Provide continuous cross-server communication.
  • Enhance user interaction through AI chatbot integration.
  • Improve and expand DinoBot's features and services.

Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. DinoBot's Python-based infrastructure allows for efficient troubleshooting and updates, ensuring a secure and reliable service.

Third-Party Services

DinoBot may integrate with third-party AI chatbots to enhance user experience. These services are carefully selected to ensure they comply with our privacy standards.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new policy on our website. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically for any updates.
